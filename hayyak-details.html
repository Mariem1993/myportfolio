HAYYEK - Internal Portal

Developed a custom internal portal for the Saudi Food and Drug Authority (SFDA) to support employee services and daily operational needs. The portal served as a centralized platform for communication, resource access, and workflow automation for staff.

Developed a custom internal portal for the Saudi Food and Drug Authority (SFDA) to streamline employee services and daily operations. The portal served as a centralized platform that improved communication, provided quick access to internal resources, and automated several workflows for staff efficiency.

  • SFDA required a single digital platform to unify employee services, resources, and communication.
  • Existing processes were manual and fragmented, leading to inefficiencies.
  • Employees faced difficulties accessing information and completing daily operational tasks in a timely manner.
  • A secure and scalable system was needed to handle internal data and ensure role-based access.

  • Designed and developed a custom Drupal-based portal tailored to SFDA’s internal requirements.
  • Implemented workflow automation to reduce manual processes and speed up internal operations.
  • Built role-based access controls to ensure secure data handling and personalized user experiences.
  • Integrated internal communication tools and resource-sharing features.
  • Delivered a responsive and user-friendly UI/UX to improve employee adoption and engagement.

Key Features

  • Employee Dashboard
  • Workflow Automation
  • Resource Management
  • Role-Based Access
  • Responsive Design
