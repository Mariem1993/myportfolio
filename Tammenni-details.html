Tammeni - Mobile Health and Drug App

View App

Led the UI/UX design for Tammeni, a consumer-facing mobile app developed by the SFDA to facilitate access to health and food safety services for the public. The app aims to provide a seamless, user-friendly experience for reporting issues, accessing information, and submitting service requests.

Tammeni is a consumer-facing mobile application developed to the Saudi Food and Drug Authority (SFDA) to improve public access to health and food safety services. The app allows users to report issues, access information, and submit service requests through an intuitive and user-friendly interface. My role focused on leading the UI/UX design, ensuring that the app is easy to navigate, visually appealing, and functional across devices.

  • Complexity of services: The app needed to cover multiple service categories, including reporting food safety issues, health-related inquiries, and regulatory information.
  • User diversity: Users ranged from tech-savvy individuals to those with minimal digital experience, requiring a highly accessible design.
  • Efficiency and clarity: Tasks such as reporting an issue or submitting a service request needed to be quick, clear, and error-free, minimizing frustration.
  • Engagement: Encouraging users to trust and regularly use the app while navigating regulatory content effectively.

  • User-centered design: Conducted user research and persona development to understand user needs, motivations, and pain points.
  • Simplified navigation: Designed a clean, intuitive interface with clear categories, step-by-step guidance, and prominent call-to-actions for critical functions.
  • Visual hierarchy & consistency: Implemented consistent UI patterns, color schemes, and typography to enhance readability and usability.
  • EInteractive prototypes: Developed clickable prototypes to test workflows and gather user feedback, iterating designs to reduce friction
  • Accessibility focus: Ensured that the app meets accessibility standards, making it usable for a wide range of users.

Key Features

  • Real-time Data Visualization
  • User Role Management
  • Secure Authentication
  • Mobile-friendly Design
  • Multi-device Support
