Business-plus- Mobile App – HR Services

View App

Designed and contributed to the development of Business-plus-, a mobile app tailored to meet HR needs by streamlining employee management, attendance tracking, and internal communication. The app enhances HR operations and improves employee engagement.

Business-plus- is a mobile application designed to simplify HR processes for organizations. It centralizes employee management, attendance tracking, and internal communication in a single platform, enhancing operational efficiency and employee engagement.

HR departments often struggle with scattered processes, manual attendance tracking, and poor internal communication, which can lead to inefficiencies, errors, and reduced employee satisfaction. There was a need for a streamlined, digital solution that could unify these processes.

Business-plus- was developed as a mobile-first platform to address these challenges. It offers a centralized dashboard for HR managers, real-time attendance monitoring, and a communication channel to keep employees informed and engaged, replacing manual systems with a digital workflow.

Key Features

  • Employee Management
  • Attendance Tracking
  • Internal Communication
  • User-Friendly Interface
